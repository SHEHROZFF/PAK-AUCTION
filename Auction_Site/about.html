About PakistanAuction

Learn more about our story, mission, and the team behind PakistanAuction.

Our Story

PakistanAuction was founded in 2018 with a simple mission: to create a premium online auction platform that connects sellers with buyers in a secure, transparent, and engaging environment.

Our founder, Michael Chen, an avid collector of vintage electronics and art, was frustrated with the limitations of existing auction platforms. He envisioned a platform that would not only provide a seamless bidding experience but also ensure that only serious bidders participated in auctions.

This led to the development of our unique 10% entry fee model, which has proven to create a more committed bidding environment and better outcomes for both buyers and sellers.

Today, PakistanAuction has grown into a thriving marketplace with thousands of successful auctions completed each month across diverse categories including antiques, electronics, art, and collectibles.

Our Mission
