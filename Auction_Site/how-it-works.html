How to Buy on PakistanAuction

A step-by-step guide to finding, bidding, and winning the auctions you love.

Buying on PakistanAuction

01. Create an Account

Sign up for a free PakistanAuction account to start browsing auctions and save your favorite items. Our simple registration process takes less than a minute to complete.

Register Now
1
2

02. Browse Auctions

Explore our extensive collection of auctions across various categories. Use our advanced filters to find exactly what you're looking for and discover unique items you won't find anywhere else.

Browse Auctions

03. Pay Entry Fee

To participate in an auction, you'll need to pay a 10% entry fee based on the item's base price. This ensures only serious bidders participate and creates a better experience for everyone.

The entry fee is non-refundable but demonstrates your commitment to the bidding process and helps maintain a quality marketplace.

3
4

04. Place Bids

Once you've paid the entry fee, you can place bids on the auction. Each auction has a minimum bid increment that you must meet or exceed to participate in the bidding process.

You'll receive real-time notifications when you're outbid so you can stay competitive and never miss an opportunity.

05. Win & Pay

If you're the highest bidder when the auction ends, congratulations! You'll receive a notification and instructions to complete your purchase within the specified timeframe.

We offer secure payment options to protect your transaction.

We'll coordinate with the seller for shipping or pickup arrangements.

01. Submit A Seller Form

Register yourself or login to enable the Selling Form. Our seller verification process ensures a trusted marketplace for all users and helps you establish credibility with potential buyers.

02. WhatsApp Notification

By submitting the form, a WhatsApp chat message will be sent to PakistanAuction management to review your product. Our team will evaluate your item and respond promptly with approval status.
